Asali Poetry Sessions

Asali is where raw voices rise. A mic, a stage, and the stories we carry, open to first-timers and returning voices alike. Each season closes with the community that has grown the room; watch our channels for the next date at Cavemen Africa, Kano.

About the night

We really want to end each season with the people who have been part of this space from the beginning. If you have attended before, come back and help us close the year together.

Microphones are shared, applause is generous, and the bar for good enough is showing up. We celebrate poets working in every language represented in the room.

Format and house etiquette

  • Sign-up list opens at the door; featured readers may be pre-booked.
  • Respect time limits so everyone gets airtime.
  • No recording without clear consent from performers.
  • Content warnings are welcome before heavier pieces.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to sign up in advance?

We recommend a quick RSVP so we can plan seating and sound. Walk-ins are welcome if space allows. You can register online before the event.

How long is each performance?

Most performance slots are around five minutes. Featured sets may run longer depending on the edition.

Is the event only for poetry?

Spoken word comes first, but storytelling and hybrid pieces often find a home here too, as long as they respect the room.

Is there a cover charge?

Contact the team for the latest ticketing or donation guidance.
